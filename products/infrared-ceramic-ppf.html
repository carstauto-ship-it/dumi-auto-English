INFRARED CERAMIC PPF

IR Heat Rejection Layer | -15°F Surface Temp | Hydrophobic | 9H Ceramic Surface

DUMI Infrared Ceramic PPF is engineered for vehicles in hot climates. Its proprietary ceramic IR rejection layer blocks up to 85% of infrared solar energy, reducing vehicle surface temperature by up to 15°F compared to unprotected paint. Combined with DUMI's signature TPU impact protection and self-healing coating, Infrared Ceramic PPF delivers the ultimate in comfort, protection, and longevity.
