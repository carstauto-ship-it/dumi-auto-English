GLASS CERAMIC TOPCOAT

Glass Ceramic Topcoat | Professional 9H Paint Surface Treatment

GLASS CERAMIC TOPCOAT is a professional-grade surface treatment delivering genuine 9H hardness protection for automotive paint. Formulated with pure glass-ceramic compounds, this topcoat creates a semi-permanent glass shield over your vehicle's paint that is harder than conventional clear coat. Repels water, dirt, bugs, bird droppings, and UV damage. The ultimate professional paint protection for show cars and daily drivers alike.
