ANTI-GRAFFITI

Anti-Graffiti Window Film | Sacrificial Protection for Glass Surfaces

ANTI-GRAFFITI is a specialized protective film designed to shield glass surfaces from graffiti, scratches, and etching damage. This 4mil thick optically-clear film installs directly over existing glass and acts as a sacrificial barrier — graffiti and scratches adhere to the film rather than the glass, allowing for easy removal and replacement. Essential for commercial vehicles, public transit, and any high-risk glass surfaces.
