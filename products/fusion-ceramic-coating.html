FUSION CERAMIC COATING

Professional Fusion Ceramic Coating | 9H Hardness Paint Sealant

FUSION CERAMIC COATING is a professional-grade ceramic coating delivering 9H surface hardness and long-lasting paint protection. Formulated with advanced SiO2 and SiC compounds, this coating chemically bonds to your vehicle paint to create an ultra-durable glass-ceramic shield that repels water, dirt, and contaminants. Lasts 3-5 years with proper maintenance — the cost-effective alternative to PPF for full-coverage protection.
