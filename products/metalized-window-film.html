METALIZED Window Film

20% VLT | Metallic Heat Rejection | High Performance | Privacy

DUMI Metalized Window Film uses precision-deposited metallic layers to reflect solar energy and reject infrared heat. At 20% VLT, this film provides excellent privacy while maintaining good visibility from inside. The metallic composition offers superior heat rejection compared to standard dyed films, making it ideal for vehicles in hot climates.
