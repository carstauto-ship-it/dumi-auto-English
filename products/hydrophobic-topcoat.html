HYDROPHOBIC TOPCOAT

Self-Cleaning Lotus Effect | PPF & Coating Maintenance | Wipe-On Application

DUMI Hydrophobic Topcoat is a professional-grade spray coating designed specifically for use on paint protection films and ceramic coatings. It creates a durable lotus-effect surface that causes water to bead and roll off, taking dirt and contaminants with it. This self-cleaning effect keeps your PPF looking pristine for longer, reduces wash frequency, and extends the life of your protection investment.
