ELECTROCHROMIC FILM

Electrochromic Smart Tint | Switchable Privacy Glass Film

ELECTROCHROMIC FILM is a revolutionary switchable smart film that transforms from clear to opaque with the touch of a button. Using advanced polymer dispersed liquid crystal (PDLC) technology, this film provides instant privacy on demand. Apply to any glass surface — windows, partitions, sunroofs — and switch between transparent and frosted privacy modes instantly.
