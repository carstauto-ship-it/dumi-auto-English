SOLAR BLOCK TINT

Solar Block Window Tint | Maximum Solar Energy Rejection Film

SOLAR BLOCK TINT is engineered specifically for maximum solar energy rejection in hot climates. Using multi-layer spectrally-selective film technology, SOLAR BLOCK rejects up to 90% of total solar energy while maintaining comfortable visibility. Reduces air conditioning load, keeps interiors cool, and protects passengers from solar heat and harmful UV. Essential for vehicles in desert and tropical regions.
