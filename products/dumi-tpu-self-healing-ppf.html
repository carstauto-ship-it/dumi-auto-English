Premium Self-Healing Paint Protection Film

DUMI TPU Self-Healing PPF is a aerospace-developed paint protection film engineered for superior automotive coverage. The heat-activated self-healing technology automatically repairs minor scratches and swirl marks, while the hydrophobic top layer prevents oxidation and maintains a factory-fresh gloss finish for up to 10 years. Universal 5ft x 16.6ft roll fits hoods, bumpers, fenders, and doors of most vehicles.
