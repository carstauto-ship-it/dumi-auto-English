PPF PROTECTOR SLEEVE

PPF Edge Protector Sleeve | Pre-Cut Edge Sealant Strips for PPF Installation

PPF PROTECTOR SLEEVE is a pre-cut edge sealant strip system designed to protect PPF edges from lifting, peeling, and contamination at vulnerable edge points. Made from a flexible rubberized compound that bonds to PPF edges, these sleeves provide a durable waterproof seal that extends PPF service life. Essential for professional installers seeking to deliver lasting, lifting-free installations.
