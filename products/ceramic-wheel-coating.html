CERAMIC WHEEL COATING

Ceramic Wheel Coating | Heat-Resistant Si02 Coating for Alloy Wheels

CERAMIC WHEEL COATING is specifically formulated for the extreme conditions that alloy wheels face — high heat, brake dust, road salt, and harsh chemicals. This Si02-based ceramic coating bonds permanently to wheel surfaces to create an ultra-hard barrier that repels brake dust, makes cleaning effortless, and protects against corrosion and oxidation. One application lasts 1-2 years.
