CARBON CERAMIC TINT

Carbon Ceramic Tint Film | Carbon + Ceramic Hybrid Heat Rejection

CARBON CERAMIC TINT combines the heat-absorbing properties of carbon film with the heat-reflecting capabilities of ceramic technology in a single hybrid construction. The carbon layer provides deep color stability and heat absorption, while the ceramic layer reflects infrared radiation. The result is balanced, effective heat rejection with a sleek dark appearance that looks factory-installed.
