HYBRID Window Film

35% VLT | Carbon + Ceramic Hybrid | 80% IR Rejection | Signal-Free

DUMI Hybrid Window Film combines the best of carbon and ceramic technologies. The carbon layer provides deep color and glare reduction while the ceramic layer delivers superior infrared heat rejection. At 35% VLT, it offers the perfect balance of privacy, style, and performance — without the premium price of full ceramic films.
