CLEAR DEFENSE

Near-Invisible Window Tint | 70% VLT Clear UV Protection Film

CLEAR DEFENSE is a virtually invisible window tint offering 70% visible light transmission while still blocking 99% of harmful UV rays and reducing solar heat. Ideal for owners who want UV protection and heat rejection without altering their vehicle's appearance. This clear film is legally permissible in most states and provides invisible protection that keeps interiors cooler and protected from sun damage.
