LUMINOUS GLOW PPF

Luminous Glow PPF | Phosphorescent Paint Protection Film

LUMINOUS GLOW PPF is a revolutionary phosphorescent paint protection film that stores light energy from the sun and emits a soft glowing effect in dark environments. During daytime, the film provides standard PPF protection. At night, the phosphorescent particles emit a subtle, elegant glow that makes your vehicle stand out.
