NANO CERAMIC PPF

9H Ceramic Hardness | Hydrophobic Surface | Next-Gen PPF Technology

DUMI Nano Ceramic PPF represents the next generation of paint protection — combining TPU base protection with an integrated nano-ceramic top layer. This breakthrough technology delivers 9H ceramic hardness on the surface, providing unmatched scratch resistance while maintaining the flexibility and impact protection of traditional PPF. The hydrophobic ceramic surface also delivers self-cleaning properties and enhanced gloss.
