STEALTH MATTE BLACK PPF

Stealth Matte Black PPF | OEM Matte Black Paint Replacement Film

STEALTH MATTE BLACK PPF transforms your vehicle into a factory-fresh matte black masterpiece. This premium matte-finish TPU film replicates the look of OEM matte black paint while providing full paint protection. Unlike paint, this film is removable and protects the original finish underneath. Loved by owners of Tesla, BMW M, and Mercedes-AMG vehicles seeking that aggressive stealth aesthetic.
