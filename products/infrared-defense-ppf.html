INFRARED DEFENSE PPF

Infrared Defense PPF | Heat-Blocking Ceramic Paint Protection Film

INFRARED DEFENSE PPF combines paint protection with advanced infrared blocking ceramic technology. This dual-function film shields your vehicle paint from stone chips and scratches while blocking infrared solar heat. The result: cooler vehicle surfaces, reduced interior temperatures, and maximum protection. Ideal for vehicles in hot climates where heat rejection and paint protection are equally important.
