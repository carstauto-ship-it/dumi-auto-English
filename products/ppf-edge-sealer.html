PPF EDGE SEALER

Prevents Edge Lifting | Moisture Barrier | Extends PPF Life

DUMI PPF Edge Sealer is a professional-grade solution designed to bond and protect PPF edges after installation. It creates a durable, invisible moisture barrier that prevents edge lifting caused by car washes, pressure washing, or environmental exposure. Formulated to be compatible with TPU and acrylic adhesives, it won't discolor or damage your PPF installation.
