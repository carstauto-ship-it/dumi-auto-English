QUICK HEAL SPRAY

Quick Heal Spray | Instant Scratch Repair Coating for PPF & Paint

QUICK HEAL SPRAY is an innovative instant scratch repair spray designed for use on both PPF and factory paint. Simply spray and wipe to activate the self-leveling formula that fills and heals minor scratches, swirl marks, and surface oxidation. The SiO2-enriched formula leaves a protective ceramic coating layer behind. Essential maintenance product for keeping PPF-covered vehicles looking pristine.
