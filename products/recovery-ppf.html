RECOVERY PPF

Recovery PPF | Multi-Layer Self-Healing Paint Protection Film

RECOVERY PPF is engineered with proprietary multi-layer self-recovery technology. Unlike conventional PPF that requires heat to heal, RECOVERY PPF can repair light surface scratches at ambient temperatures through its unique elastomeric topcoat formulation. For deeper scratches, simple sun exposure accelerates the healing process. An essential upgrade for daily drivers seeking perpetually pristine paint protection.
