Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About PPF & Window Tint

What is Paint Protection Film (PPF)?

Paint Protection Film (PPF) is a transparent urethane film applied to a vehicle's paint surface to protect it from rock chips, scratches, road debris, and environmental damage. PPF features self-healing technology that repairs minor scratches with heat.

How long does PPF last?

Our premium PPF comes with a 10-year warranty against yellowing, cracking, peeling, and delamination.. With proper care, Our PPF can protect your vehicle's paint for 10+ years while maintaining its clarity and gloss.

Does ceramic coating protect better than PPF?

PPF and ceramic coating serve different purposes. PPF provides physical protection against impacts and scratches (8-10mil thickness), while ceramic coating provides chemical protection, enhanced gloss, and hydrophobic properties. Many customers choose both for complete protection.

What is the difference between ceramic tint and regular tint?

Ceramic window tint uses nano-ceramic particles to block infrared heat and UV rays without interfering with radio signals (GPS, cell phone, satellite radio). Compared to dyed or metallic tints, ceramic tint offers superior heat rejection, no signal interference, and longer-lasting performance.
